About us

Our School Motto 'By Faith and Work', and our School Rule 'Be Sensible and Think First of Others', encapsulate what our staff, children, governors and parents work towards in our school. We work especially hard to make sure that children feel safe and happy at our school and provide children with a stimulating and exciting environment in which to learn.

We are committed to safeguarding and promoting the welfare of children and young people and expect all staff and volunteers to share this commitment. As a islamic School, our core values of Truth, Fairness, Justice and Joy define us not only as members of AL-Helal Islami Academy, but also of the wider islamic family whose values underpin everything we do. These values are also used daily as they are linked to our reward system. We celebrate many religious festivals there regularly.

If you are a prospective parent/carer and would like more information about a place for your child, please contact our school office to speak with a member of staff who will be delighted to arrange a visit and take you on a tour of our wonderful school.
